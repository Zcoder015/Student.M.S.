About Our System

The Student Management System is designed to simplify the process of managing students, teachers, and courses. It provides a fast and intuitive way of tracking attendance, progress, grades, and other crucial aspects of a student's academic journey all in one place.

This system is perfect for small to medium-sized schools or educational institutions that require a less complex solution that is both affordable and easy to use.

Features

        Fast and easy student registration
        Effortless management of multiple courses and teachers
        Attendance tracking and management
        Automated grading and progress tracking
        Security and data protection
